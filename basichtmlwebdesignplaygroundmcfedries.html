Web video formats. WebM .webm is open source and royalty free uses VP8 or VP9 video codec and Vorbis or Opus audio codec. Ogg .ogg or .ogv is open source and royalty free uses Theora video codec and Vorbis or Opus audio codec. MPEG-4 .mp4 patented yet free for end users uses H.264 video codec and AAC audio codec.
